The best of Kerico for your business

Kerico Healthcare provides Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) services for organizations and thousands of rides annually.

Kerico for all your business needs

  • Clinics and medical providers

    We help clinics improve their appointment show rates by offering an affordable ride scheduling and requesting solution for their patients who may not have easy access to transportation.

  • Retireement homes and communities

    We provide retirement homes and residential care facilities with an affordable and flexible transportation to help their residents stay connected to their the community for trips to shopping, entertainment, recreation, the arts, and family visits.

  • Skilled nursing facilities

    Our highly screened group of drivers with specialized health care training only drive for us. This ensures residents arrive safely to their medical appointments on time with any assistance or help needed.
