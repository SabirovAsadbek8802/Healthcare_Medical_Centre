What you need to drive for Kerico:

01 - Apply Online

Enter your contact information into the form above and you'll be on your way to becoming a Veyo Driver. In most cases, our onboarding process takes 3-7 days, and it only takes a few minutes to get started.

02 - Upload Documents

Tell us what car you drive, upload your drivers license, insurance & registration, and we'll start your background check. No need to find a fax machine, you can use the camera on your phone.

03 - Get Credentialed

All Drivers need to complete a vehicle inspection, watch the training videos, complete a CPR Certification and provide payment information. We'll walk you through each step.
