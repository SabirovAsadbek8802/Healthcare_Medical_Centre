About Kerico

Kerico Healthcare provides Non-Emergency Medical Transportation
(NEMT) services for organizations and thousands of rides annually.
We are trusted by hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers to get
their patients to and from their medical appointments with great
customer experience.

Kerico service

Clinics and medical providers

We help clinics improve their appointment show
rates by offering an affordable ride scheduling
and requesting solution for their patients who
may not have easy access to transportation.

Retireement homes and communities

We provide retirement homes and residential
care facilities with an affordable and flexible
transportation to help their residents stay
connected to their the community for trips to
shopping, entertainment, recreation, the arts,
and family visits.

Skilled nursing facilities

Our highly screened group of drivers with
specialized health care training only drive for us.
This ensures residents arrive safely to their
medical appointments on time with any
assistance or help needed.

"Making it easier for our patients to get a ride to and from
their home improved our appointment show rate and our
facility utilization rate. That in turn has improved patient
satisfaction and our bottom line."

Maulik Patel, CFO
